Oracle's Wisdom (FAQ)

Everything you and your guardians might want to know about EpicRush and your epic quests.

Is EpicRush a safe realm for young heroes?

Absolutely! We focus on forging a non-violent, age-appropriate realm for young adventurers to quest and explore.

Will it function on my ancient tablet?

Indeed! Our quests are designed to function perfectly on tablets, scrolls, and arcane computers using any modern browser.

Must I download any ancient texts?

Nay! All our quests unfold directly in your web browser. No installations or visits to the arcane app market needed.

Are there any hidden tolls or curses?

Our quests are free to embark upon! We wish for everyone to enjoy the adventure without worry.

How are legendary scores recorded?

Your legendary scores are etched right on your own device, so you can witness your progress upon your next visit.

Can guardians send us scrolls?

Yes! We welcome wisdom from guardians. You can reach out through our contact page or send us a raven directly.

What are the quest controls?

On an arcane computer, use the Arrow keys or A/D to traverse. On a tablet or scroll, simply drag your finger to move!

Can I quest alongside my companions?

While our current quests are solo expeditions, you can challenge your companions to surpass your legendary score!

Do the quests aid young heroes in gaining wisdom?

Our quests focus on developing hand-eye coordination, swift thinking, and problem-solving skills.

How frequently are new quests added to the realms?

We are always forging new ideas and hope to add more thrilling adventures frequently!

Is my young hero's data protected by ancient wards?

We hold privacy sacred. We do not collect personal information from our young adventurers.

What if I encounter a glitch in the realm?

If something is amiss in the realm, please let us know via the contact form so we can mend it for all!
