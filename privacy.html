Privacy Scroll

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Welcome to EpicRush. Your privacy is a sacred oath to us. We are committed to being transparent about how we gather, utilize, and safeguard your information. This scroll is penned in plain language to help you comprehend our practices. Our aim is to collect only the data essential to provide and enhance our multiplayer quests and guild features, granting you full dominion over your information.

1. Information We Gather

To run our services, we gather a minimal amount of information. Here’s what we gather and why:

  • Hero Profile (Optional): If you forge an account, we may gather a hero name, email address, and password. We use this to preserve your progress, manage your companion list, and secure your account. You are not required to forge an account to embark on many of our quests.
  • Quest Data: We automatically gather data about your in-quest activity, such as your scores, achievements, quest session length, and interactions with other heroes in a quest. This helps us balance quests, mend glitches, and power features like the Scroll of Legends.
  • Hero-Generated Content: We gather information you voluntarily provide, such as messages in quest chat, guild hall posts, or your public hero profile. Please be mindful not to share personal information in public-facing guild spaces.
  • Contact Information: If you send us a scroll directly via our contact form or raven, we will have a record of our correspondence, including your name and email address, to provide you with guidance.
  • Technical & Usage Data: We gather anonymous or aggregated data about how you interact with our realms, such as your browser type, device type, realms visited, and time spent in the realms. We use this information to understand traffic, improve realm performance, and ensure compatibility. This is gathered via standard server logs and minimal, privacy-focused analytics.

2. How We Employ Your Information

Our primary goal is to provide a thrilling and safe questing environment. We employ your information to:

  • Operate and maintain our quests and realms.
  • Enable quest features, such as preserving progress.
  • Display the Scroll of Legends and personal high scores.
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., by showing your hero name).
  • Communicate with you for support or to respond to feedback.
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve our services and develop new quests.
  • Enforce our safety policies to ensure a respectful environment for all young heroes.

3. Sharing Data and Other Guilds

We do not trade your personal information. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information if you give us explicit permission to do so.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.
  • Service Guilds: We may use third-party guilds for services like realm hosting or analytics. These providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to protect it.

4. Your Rights and Decrees

You hold dominion over your personal information. You have the right to:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your hero profile through your settings (if applicable).
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated personal lore by contacting us.
  • Clear Local Data: You can clear locally stored data, such as legendary scores saved in your browser, by clearing your browser's arcane memory for our site.
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You can unsubscribe from any herald's pronouncements we might send in the future.

5. Data Wards

We employ reasonable wards to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or revelation. However, no internet-based realm is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute safety.

6. Young Heroes' Privacy

Our services are not directed to young heroes under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly gather personal information from young heroes. If we learn that we have, we will take steps to erase such information promptly.

7. Contact the Sages

If you have any queries or concerns about this Privacy Scroll or our data practices, please do not hesitate to contact the Sages at privacy@epicrush.com. We are here to guide you.
